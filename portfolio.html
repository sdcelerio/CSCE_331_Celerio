200Wh Battery Pack

Ongoing since Spring 2024

A 3D printed battery pack with 15 18650 Li-Ion batteries that can provide up to 17.5 Ah of power, which can power up to 4 phones. The battery pack features 4 USB-A ports, a 150 Watt AC wall port, wireless phones charging, and a variable DC port. To function, the circuit ultilizes multiple relays, a power-inverter to convert DC to AC power, DC buck converter with constant current, and a BMS (Battery management system) chip to safetly charge the batteries.

Microcontroller w/ Sensor PCB

Spring 2024 - Summer 2024

This PCB design was engineered for the "Mouseless Mouse", a glove-like device that functions like a mouse. Furthermore, the device supports app functionality and a wireless connection through bluetooth. A PCB design with a SEEED Studio Xiao Microcontroller combined with a BNO086 sensor. While powered, the sensor will send data to the microcontroller, which performs specific calculations and inputs to the machine it is connected to.
